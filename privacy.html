Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters: Horror Apps Studio is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your information when you use the Momo Horror Challenge application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use Momo Horror Challenge, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interaction patterns, session duration, feature usage statistics
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, app performance metrics, error logs
  • Approximate Location: General geographic region (city/country level) for content optimization

1.2 Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when using certain features:

  • Feedback and Support: When you contact us or report issues
  • User Preferences: Your customization settings and horror intensity preferences
  • Contact Information: If you choose to provide it for support purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Momo Horror Challenge experience
  • Personalize horror scenarios based on your preferences and device capabilities
  • Analyze app performance and fix technical issues
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Deliver relevant advertisements through our advertising partners
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. Third-Party Services

Momo Horror Challenge integrates with the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and core functionality
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Firebase Analytics: For usage analytics and crash reporting
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For identifying and fixing app crashes
  • Unity Ads: Additional advertising platform
  • AppLovin: Advertising and monetization services

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing how they use your data. We encourage you to review their policies.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure data storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Strict internal policies for data access and handling

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
